About

Me

I love nature, I love being in nature, and especially doing sports outside. This gives me peace and helps me to recreate after work. I tried out many sports, currently my favourites are hiking and race biking. Two sports of quite some difference in their speed. Well, hiking I do love, because you can focus on the surroundings, on the plants blooming around you, and eventually on mushrooms to be collected ;) Race biking is great for focusing on the body itself, the rythm of breath, because nature just flys by.

Early in school, in biology class, I had to collect plants from my hometown for my first herbarium. I simply loved it! Maybe that was one of the catalysts for studying Biology | Botany in Vienna. With minor excursions I stayed in science and currently I am working as a plant scientist (Me @ work). As a scientist data are worth one's weight in gold! This brought me to enrolling in a Master study in bioinformatics. And here I am!
